# Outreach templates (EN + 繁中)

Rules: short, specific, no hype. Lead with their world (nurse-led chronic care),
not your tech. One ask: a 20-minute demo. Attach/link the 85s video. Find the
named person on LinkedIn first; personalise line 1.

---

**Subject:** Between-visit monitoring for your chronic-care patients (20-min look?)

### Do / don't
- **Do** name a real program of theirs in line 1. **Do** keep the ask to 20 min.
- **Do** always include "monitoring support, not diagnosis."
- **Don't** lead with "AI". **Don't** attach a deck on first touch — the video + a
  demo offer converts better. **Don't** claim outcomes you don't have yet
  (the dashboard numbers are demo data — say so if asked).
