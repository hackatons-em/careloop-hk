# Hong Kong — first-customer target shortlist

> Goal: land **one design partner** for a pilot. Lead with the live demo + the
> 85s video; you need no entity/WABA to demo. Contact routes are
> department/public-level — find the named person on LinkedIn before sending.

## The strategic wedge — ride a scheme the government already funds
HK is actively paying for community chronic-disease management with **nurse
follow-up**:
- **District Health Centres (DHCs)** — one per district (full DHC or "DHC
  Express"), **operated by NGOs via government tender**; Category-2 services
  explicitly include chronic-disease management + diabetes/hypertension screening.
- **Chronic Disease Co-Care (CDCC) Pilot Scheme** — public-private; the Health
  Bureau has procured **nurse clinic + allied-health services at 40+ points**,
  many in the **private** sector, for chronic-disease management.
- **Nurse-led clinics** (incl. heart-failure) already exist in HK with a
  published evidence base for cutting readmissions.

**Miruwa's pitch into this:** "You already run nurse-led chronic-care
follow-up. Miruwa is the *between-visit* layer — daily WhatsApp/Cantonese
voice check-ins, a deterministic engine that surfaces only the patients who need
a nurse, and the outcome data to report against the scheme." You're not creating
a new behaviour; you're making an existing, funded one more efficient.

## Tier 1 — start here (mission-fit + reachable for a solo founder)
| Target | Why it fits | Contact route |
|---|---|---|
| **Haven of Hope Christian Service (靈實)** | Runs **Community Nursing**, Community Geriatric, and a respiratory Nurse & Allied-Health clinic across ~46 sites; explicitly chronic-disease + elderly focused. Near-perfect ICP. | hohcs.org.hk; HKCSS directory (ngo.hkcss.org.hk/ngo/166); LinkedIn: Service Director / Head of Community Health |
| **A DHC operator in one district** | NGO-operated, chronic-disease mandate, has the patient flow + nurses. Pick the operator of one district's DHC. | dhc.gov.hk → healthcare service providers → district operator; approach that NGO's DHC centre manager |
| **CDCC private providers** — e.g. **Quality HealthCare (QHMS)**, **Human Health Holdings** | Already procured to run CDCC nurse clinics; commercial, fast-moving, KPI-driven. | qhms.com / humanhealth.com.hk → corporate / clinical-operations contact |

## Tier 3 — later (need references first)
| Target | Note |
|---|---|
| **Residential Care Homes (RCHEs)** — Pine Care, Culture Homes, NGO-run homes | High chronic load, but slower tech adoption; better once you have proof. |
| **Hospital Authority** clusters (Community Nursing Service, Nurse & Allied-Health Clinics, cardiac/geriatric depts) | Biggest prize, slowest procurement. Approach via HA innovation channels once you have a pilot to show. |

## Warm-intro accelerators (use these — solo founders get meetings via ecosystem, not cold)
- **HKSTP** (Science Park) & **Cyberport** health-tech communities — many of these
  NGOs/clinics partner there; incubation can get you warm intros + credibility.
- **HKCSS** (Council of Social Service) — the umbrella for the NGOs above; its
  directory (`ngo.hkcss.org.hk`) is your map.
- University centres (e.g. CUHK Health Systems & Policy Research) run DHC studies —
  good for an academic/clinical champion.
