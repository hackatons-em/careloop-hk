# 20-minute demo script + checklist

Goal of the call: get a **"yes, let's run a small pilot."** Not to show every
feature — to make them feel the between-visit gap close.

## Before the call (checklist)
- [ ] Live demo logged in and working: `careloop-hk.vercel.app` (demo data loaded).
- [ ] A phone ready to send a WhatsApp voice note into the demo (sandbox) so the
      check-in appears live — the "wow" moment.
- [ ] The 85s video open in a tab.
- [ ] The one-pager ready to send after.
- [ ] Know their program: which conditions, how their nurses currently follow up.
- [ ] Silence your other notifications; share a clean screen.

## The flow (≈20 min)
**1. Their world (2 min) — ask, don't pitch.**
"Walk me through what happens to a heart-failure patient between visits today?"
Let them describe the gap. Mirror it back: "so between visits, you're mostly blind."

**2. The 85s film (1.5 min).** Play it. It sets the frame so you don't have to.

**3. Live product (9 min) — the spine:**
- **Patient side:** send a Cantonese voice note from your phone → show it land,
  transcribed and understood, on the dashboard. *"Your patient does nothing new —
  it's just WhatsApp."*
- **Triage:** the dashboard — "one queue, only the patients who need a nurse, not
  200 charts." Open the flagged patient.
- **Explainable alert:** show the exact rule + evidence. *"Deterministic — same
  data in, same result out. Never an AI diagnosis. Your nurse stays in charge."*
- **Trends:** weight climbing past baseline → "caught early, not at readmission."
- **Caregiver alert:** consent-gated message to family.
- **Program view:** response rate, alert volume, time-to-acknowledge + the board PDF.
  *"This is what you'd report against the CDCC / to your board."*

**4. Safety & fit (3 min).** Monitoring, not a medical device; PDPO-aligned;
one isolated instance per org; full audit trail; bilingual. Pre-empt the
"is this an AI making decisions?" fear: no — it's a fixed, auditable rule set.

**5. The ask (4 min).** "Could we run a 12-week pilot with ~[20–50] of your
patients, measured on a ≥70% check-in response rate? I'll set everything up; your
nurses just use the queue. What would you need to say yes?"
Then: agree a next step + date. Send the one-pager + pilot outline same day.

## Objection handling
- **"Is the AI diagnosing?"** → No. A deterministic rule engine flags; your nurse
  decides. You can read every rule on the Rules page.
- **"Patient privacy / PDPO?"** → One isolated instance, consent-gated, full audit,
  data export/delete on request. Here's the DPA.
- **"Our patients aren't tech-savvy."** → It's just WhatsApp, and they can reply by
  *voice* in Cantonese. Nothing to install.
- **"Do you have results?"** → Be honest: the dashboard figures are demo data;
  that's exactly what the pilot measures. You're offering to prove it on their patients.
- **"Are you a real company?"** → Honest: early, founder-led, that's why I'm
  offering a hands-on design partnership — you shape it and get it first.

## After the call
- Same day: thank-you + one-pager + 3-line pilot outline (scope, metric, what you
  need from them) + a proposed start date.
- Log it. Follow up in 4–5 days if quiet.
