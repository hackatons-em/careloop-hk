## 1. Purpose
A time-limited, paid pilot of the Miruwa remote between-visit monitoring
service, to evaluate fit for the Customer's chronic-care population before any
longer-term agreement.

## 2. Scope
- **Patients:** up to [50] enrolled patients.
- **Conditions:** [heart failure / COPD / hypertension / diabetes / post-stroke / kidney disease].
- **Term:** [12] weeks from go-live, then month-to-month unless a subscription
  is signed.
- **Channel:** daily WhatsApp check-ins (incl. Cantonese voice notes), nurse
  dashboard, caregiver alerts (consent-gated), program analytics.
- A dedicated isolated instance per §Security of the DPA.

## 3. Success criteria
- Primary: **≥ 70%** patient check-in response rate over the pilot, measured by
  the Program analytics from recorded data only (no projections).
- Secondary (review, not pass/fail): time-to-acknowledge on escalations;
  qualitative nurse feedback.

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Provider:** provision and host the instance; provide onboarding + a 30-min
  nurse walkthrough; maintain availability and support during the pilot;
  process data only per the DPA.
- **Customer:** designate an administrator; enrol patients and obtain the
  necessary patient/caregiver consents; ensure clinical staff act on alerts per
  their own clinical judgement.

## 5. Clinical positioning (important)
Miruwa is **monitoring support, not a medical device, and does not provide
diagnosis or treatment**. All clinical decisions rest with the Customer's
licensed staff. Severity is decided by a deterministic, auditable rule engine —
not by AI.
