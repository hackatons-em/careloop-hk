# CareLoop HK — Pitch Slides

Six clean slides. Tagline: **CareLoop watches the gaps between visits.**

---

## Slide 1 — Title

# CareLoop HK
### Remote chronic-care monitoring for elderly patients between visits

Daily check-ins, vital signals, rule-based escalation, and nurse review.

---

## Slide 2 — Problem

**Elderly chronic-care patients deteriorate between visits.**

Families and care teams often miss early signals until the patient needs urgent care.

Early signals that get missed:

- Sudden weight gain (fluid retention)
- Shortness of breath
- Missed medication
- Low activity
- High blood pressure

---

## Slide 3 — Product

**CareLoop turns daily signals into nurse action.**

1. Daily Cantonese check-in
2. Wearable / vital data
3. Rule-based risk alerts (deterministic, explainable)
4. Nurse review queue
5. Caregiver alert (English + 繁體中文)
6. Weekly clinician summary
7. FHIR-style export

---

## Slide 4 — Demo case

**Mrs. Chan, 78 — heart failure + hypertension**

Signals this week:

- +2.3 kg in 3 days
- Shortness of breath
- Swelling
- Missed medication

Result: rule engine matches **HF-001** and **HF-002** → **Escalate** to nurse review.

> CareLoop does not ask an LLM to decide severity. The rule engine is deterministic and auditable.

Hong Kong faces ageing and chronic-care pressure, and its health system is moving toward stronger
primary care and eHealth interoperability.

CareLoop supports between-visit monitoring and eHealth-style interoperability **without replacing
clinicians**.
